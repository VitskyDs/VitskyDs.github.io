AceTech


AceTech is a a fully integrated medical care company at the forefront of supportive healthcare. They produce medical products based on the Acemanan molecule, which is extracted from aloe grown by the company in Cost Rica.

Visit Website

Strategy

The goal was to create a website that introduces the company to potential partners and generates leads for sales.

AceTech is unique in the field of supportive care - the vertical nature of the company - complete control of the process, from plantation to final product, the natural ingredient they base their products on, and the exclusive extraction process by which they obtain the acemanan molecule set them apart from competitors.

We’ve decided to design an engaging and beautiful website, while maintaining a professinal and medical feel, with emphasis on technology, showcasing the science and techonolgy behind the company.
