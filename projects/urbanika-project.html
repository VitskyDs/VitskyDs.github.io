Watoobi

Urban Guide for the city lovers. Through virtual navigation within the city and exploration of key buildings the user gets a sense of the city's vast architecture.

Illustrated chapters tell one of many stories of the city and offer a subjective perspective on a specific subject, in this case Jerusalem's expansion beyond the Old City walls.

The two graphic styles differntiate between the two points of view - the objective and the subjective.

Graduation project, Bezalel 2015.
